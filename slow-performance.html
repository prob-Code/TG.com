1. Restart Your Device

Restarting your device can clear temporary files and stop unnecessary processes that may be slowing it down.

2. Free Up Storage Space

Check your storage usage and remove unnecessary files or apps to free up space, which can improve performance.

3. Close Background Apps

Close apps running in the background that may be using up system resources and affecting performance.

4. Check for Software Updates

Ensure your device's operating system and apps are up-to-date. Updates often include performance improvements and bug fixes.

5. Perform a Factory Reset

If performance issues persist, consider performing a factory reset. This will restore your device to its original settings and remove all data, so back up important files first.
